He is a gem in the world of stand-up comedy, began his journey in Dallas, Texas. Born to Malayali parents, Varghese’s cultural background played a pivotal role in shaping his unique comedy style.

Varghese’s comedic talent burst onto the national scene with his participation in Last Comic Standing 2, where his humor resonated with audiences, propelling him to the semi-finals.
