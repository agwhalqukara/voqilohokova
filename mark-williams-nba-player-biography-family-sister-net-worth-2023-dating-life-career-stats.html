Mark Williams: Career Details

  • Williams was named to the Kareem Abdul-Jabbar Award preseason watch list prior to the start of the season. He scored 11 points and grabbed 5 rebounds in a 79-53 win over Clemson on January 30, 2021.
  • Williams had 13 points and 5 blocks in a 69-53 victory over NC State on February 13, 2021. He had 18 points and 11 rebounds in an 85-71 win over Syracuse on February 22, 2021.
  • Williams got another double-double of 23 points and 19 rebounds in a 70-56 victory over Louisville in the second round of the ACC tournament on March 10, 2021.
  • In that game, his 19 rebounds set a record for the most ever by an ACC Tournament freshman, surpassing former Virginia center and NBA Hall of Famer Ralph Sampson’s 18 rebounds against Clemson in the 1980 ACC Tournament and placing third all-time at Duke.
  • Williams averaged 7.1 points and 4.5 rebounds per game as a freshman.
  • He set a career high with 28 points against Syracuse on February 26, 2022. As a sophomore, Williams was selected ACC Defensive Player of the Year and Third Team All-ACC.
  • He averaged 11.2 points, 7.4 rebounds, and 2.8 blocks per game as a sophomore. Williams declared for the 2022 NBA Draft on April 18, 2022, foregoing his remaining college eligibility.
  • The Charlotte Hornets selected Williams with the 15th overall choice in the 2022 NBA draft.
