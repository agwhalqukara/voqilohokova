Bhad Bhabie rang in her 20th birthday this week and celebrated with a big party that included giving a lap dance – to her own mother.

According to TMZ, the “Gucci Flip Flops” rapper flew on a private jet to her home state of Florida for her big day on Sunday (March 26). Celebrating with close friends and family at her house, she clearly had quite the time.
